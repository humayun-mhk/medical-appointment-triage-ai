# Frequently Asked Questions

This document supports clinic and routing questions only.

## Does the AI diagnose disease?

No. The system does not diagnose disease, prescribe medicine, or replace a doctor. It only helps collect symptoms, detect safety red flags, recommend a doctor specialty, and prepare a doctor-facing summary.

## Can I book directly without triage?

Yes. Patients can browse doctors and book available slots manually. AI triage is optional routing support.

## What happens if emergency symptoms are detected?

The system shows an emergency warning and recommends seeking immediate emergency care. It should not treat emergency symptoms as a routine appointment.

## Can I cancel an appointment?

Booked appointments can be cancelled from the patient appointment history. Cancelled slots may become available again.

## What should I bring to the appointment?

Bring previous reports, current medication list, identification, and clear symptom notes. The doctor makes the final medical judgment.
