# Clinic Appointment Policy

This document supports appointment workflow guidance only.

Patients can book available doctor slots shown in the system. A slot can be booked only once. If a slot is already booked, the patient should select another available time.

Appointments can be cancelled by the patient while the status is booked. When an appointment is cancelled, the slot returns to available status unless clinic staff decide otherwise.

Rescheduling is handled by cancelling the existing appointment and booking a new available slot.

Patients should arrive early enough to complete registration and share symptom information clearly. Late arrivals may need to wait or reschedule depending on clinic policy and doctor availability.

Emergency symptoms should not wait for routine appointment workflow. Patients with emergency red flags should seek emergency care immediately.
