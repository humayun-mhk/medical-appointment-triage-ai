# Patient Preparation Guidelines

This document supports safe appointment preparation guidance only.

Patients should bring previous reports, relevant imaging, lab reports, discharge summaries, and referral notes if available.

Patients should bring a current medication list, including names and doses, so the doctor can review it. The system should not recommend starting, stopping, or changing medicine.

Patients should arrive early, keep identification and payment information ready, and share symptoms clearly with duration, severity, associated symptoms, and any emergency warning signs.

Patients should tell the doctor about allergies, chronic conditions, pregnancy status, and previous major illnesses or surgeries.

If symptoms include emergency red flags, patients should seek emergency care immediately instead of waiting for a routine appointment.
